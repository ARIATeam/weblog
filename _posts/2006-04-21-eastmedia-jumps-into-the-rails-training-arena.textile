Matt Pelletier's "EastMedia":http://www.eastmedia.com/, a Rails-centric firm out of New York City, has announced that they are offering "two Rails classes":http://eastmedia.com/event/list in the near future.

The first is a "one day course in New York City":http://eastmedia.com/event/index/1 on April 29th taught by Matt Pelletier and Francis Hwang, the founder of the NYC Ruby user group, humanist and author of, among other things, "Lafcadio":http://lafcadio.rubyforge.org/ and "MockFS":http://mockfs.rubyforge.org/. Francis has been building websites in Ruby for over 4 years now. Up until recently he was the primary developer for "http://rhizome.org/":http://rhizome.org/. You can "sign up here":http://eastmedia.com/event/index/1 for $395.
