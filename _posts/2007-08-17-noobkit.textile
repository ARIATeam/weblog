Looking for a good way to browse the Rails API documentation? Alex Gorbatchev has recently created "Noobkit":http://www.noobkit.com/, a documentation browser for Rails, Ruby core, and over 20 other useful libraries. It also supports searching, comments (with your OpenID), and bookmarks.
