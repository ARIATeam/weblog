Rails is built upon the foundation of a model-view-control split of responsibilities. The MVC pattern can be quite bewildering if you're coming from procedural PHP, other non-OOP environments, or are just starting out with programming. But now you can learn it all. Amy Hoy has written a great introduction to the pattern and how it relates to Rails: <a href="http://www.slash7.com/flashback/2005/02/mvc_the_most_ve.html">MVC: The Most Vexing Conundrum</a>.
