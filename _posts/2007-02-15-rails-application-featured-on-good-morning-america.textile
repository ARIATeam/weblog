"InfoQ":http://infoq.com recently conducted an "interview":http://www.infoq.com/news/2007/02/revolution-health-profile with the developers that discusses, among other things, their "PluGems":http://revolutiononrails.blogspot.com/2007/01/plugems-rails-as-first-class-citizens.html (it's a plugin! it's a gem!!) approach to sharing code across the multiple applications that make up Revolution Health.
