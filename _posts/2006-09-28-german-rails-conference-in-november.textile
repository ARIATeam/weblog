"rails-konferenz.de":http://rails-konferenz.de/ is a one-day German Rails conference taking place at the NH Hotel in Frankfurt am Main on November 3rd. They have "sessions":http://rails-konferenz.de/programm.html on localization, testing, backgroundrb, and more. 

If you speak German and have Frankfurt within reach, you should check it out.
