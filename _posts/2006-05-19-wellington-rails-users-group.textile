Tomek Piatek and others have started Well Railed, the Rails User's Group in Wellington, NZ. The first meeting will be on the 30th May at 6:30-ish at "Hell Pizza in Bond Street":http://www.zoomin.co.nz/nz/wellington/te+aro/bond+street/14/-syn+bar+hell+pizza/.
