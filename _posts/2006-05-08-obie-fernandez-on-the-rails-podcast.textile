In this installment of the "Rails podcast":http://podcast.rubyonrails.org, "Geoffrey Grosenbach":http://nubyonrails.com catches up with "Obie Fernandez":http://www.jroller.com/page/obie of "Thoughtworks":http://thoughtworks.com. 

Obie has been using Rails with great success to take on "enterprise" scale projects. A die hard Java programmer since 1996, he admits that his first impression of Ruby was that "he hated it":http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Denial.
