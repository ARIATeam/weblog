Long time resident mentor on "ruby-talk":http://www.ruby-talk.org and "Ruby Central":http://www.rubycentral.org co-founder, "David Black":http://www.rubypowerandlight.com,  is just finishing up a book that should be of special interest to those whose first Ruby experiences are coming by way of Rails: "Ruby for Rails - Ruby techniques for Rails developers":http://manning.com/books/black.
