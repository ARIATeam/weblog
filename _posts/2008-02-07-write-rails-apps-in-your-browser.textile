"Heroku":http://heroku.com/ is a rails platform with a twist: it all runs in your browser. 

The entire platform is build around a web based IDE which allows full rails development and deployment. Your apps are deployed to "Amazon's Elastic Cloud":http://www.amazon.com/gp/browse.html?node=201590011 by the push of a button.
